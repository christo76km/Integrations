-- TODO Materialized “effective scrobbles” cache table (optional but powerful)
Your queries frequently compute “effective” artist/album/track by joining scrobble_updates. For larger datasets + many updates, you can maintain a cache table:

effective_scrobbles_cache(scrobble_id, artist, album, track, last_update_ts)
update it whenever an override is inserted (single scrobble or bulk)
then your main queries become much simpler and faster (no window functions every time)

Benefit: dramatic speedups as scrobble_updates grows.
